History

HISTORY

History

CoorS is a historical brand. It is established in 1978, but before that, the founder Mr. Coors has already opened a brewery in 1950s. His son, Mr. Coors the junior, inherited the property and named the brewery after his family name. Since then, CoorS opened plants across the country and gradually became one of the mainstream brand in Canada beer market. In 2010, CoorS acquired Gas-X, putting emphasize on improving healthy lifestyle.
